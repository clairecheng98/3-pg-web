Lives under Coronavirus: Visual and Written Components

Since its outbreak in December 2019, the novel Coronavirus (COVID-19) has continued to spread. As of February 15, the global death toll has reached over 1,500 people, with more than 66,000 people infected in 25 other countries. People put on protective clothes such as a face mask when walking on streets. The virus is one thing, they said, but fear of the virus is another epidemic by itself.
